Mahatma Gandhi

Father of the Nation


Photo: Gandhi in 1947, with Lord Louis Mountbatten, Britain's last Viceroy of India, and his wife Edwina Mountbatten.

Here's a brief chronology of Mahatma Gandhi's life:

  • 2nd October,1869 - Born at Porbandar, Kathiawad.
  • 1883 - Married Kasturbai, aged 13.
  • 1888 - Leaves for England to study law.
  • 1893 - Worked as a lawyer in South Africa.
  • 1914 - Returned to India.
  • 30 January,1948 - Assasinated because of his beliefs.
